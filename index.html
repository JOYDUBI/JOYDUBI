top places to go in paris

  1. L'Arpege
  2. Musee Rodin
  3. Les Invalides

When you do visit Paris, you absolutely must visit L’Arpège. This restaurant was voted one of the best in the whole city for it's exquisite vegetarian dishes.

After your superb dinner, take a short walk to the Musee Rodin. This museum will leave you in awe of the art work and history behind each piece

Next, just take a short stroll to your closest hotel, Les Invalides. Know for it's exemplary customer service, you won't have anything to worry about on your trip.
